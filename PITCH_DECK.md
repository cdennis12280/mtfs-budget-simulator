## Slide 3: The Solution

**Headline:** From Static Models to Strategic Insight

**Introducing:** The MTFS Budget Simulator, a web-based, interactive tool that transforms your financial planning.

**Core Value Proposition (in 3 key blocks):**

1.  **Instant "What-If" Analysis:** Model the impact of changes to council tax, grants, pay awards, and demand pressures in real-time.
2.  **Integrated Capital Planning:** Instantly see the revenue impact of your capital programme on the MTFS budget gap and reserves.
3.  **Clear Communication:** Use dynamic charts and RAG ratings to clearly communicate financial sustainability to leadership and members.

## Slide 4: Key Feature: The Capital Impact Engine

**Headline:** Bridge the Gap Between Capital Ambition and Revenue Reality

**Visual:** A diagram showing Capital Inputs -> **[Calculator]** -> Revenue Outputs.

**How it Works:**

1.  **Input Project Details:** Enter a capital project's value and financing method (borrowing, grants, etc.).
2.  **Auto-Calculate Costs:** The simulator instantly calculates the annual MRP and interest costs.
3.  **See the Impact:** The new revenue costs are automatically fed into the 5-year MTFS, updating the budget gap and all associated KPIs.

**Key Benefit:** "Finally, a clear, immediate answer to the question: 'What is the true revenue cost of this capital project?'"
