# Walkthrough Video Guide (3–5 minutes)

> Quick links: `docs/QUICK_START.md` for setup/run, `docs/DEPLOYMENT.md` for hosting, `docs/AUTHENTICATION.md` for access control.


## Recording Setup
- Use screen capture at 1080p.
- Start on the Dashboard.
- Keep cursor movements minimal.

## Suggested Flow
1. **Dashboard Overview (30s)**
   - Point to S151 Summary strip, RAG, and 4-year gap.
2. **Scenario Story (30s)**
   - Highlight Base vs Pessimistic comparison.
3. **Sensitivity (45s)**
   - Open `Sensitivity` and show tornado chart.
4. **Risk Advisor (45s)**
   - Show risk register linkage and composite stress.
5. **Reports (60s)**
   - Generate Executive Summary and Board Pack.
6. **Audit (30s)**
   - Open `Audit` and show export log entries.

## Closing Line
“This provides a transparent, auditable MTFS evidence trail for Section 151 governance.”
