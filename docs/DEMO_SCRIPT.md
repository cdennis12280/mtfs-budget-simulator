# Live Demo Script (5–7 minutes)

> Quick links: `docs/QUICK_START.md` for setup/run, `docs/DEPLOYMENT.md` for hosting, `docs/AUTHENTICATION.md` for access control.


## 0. Setup (30 seconds)
- Enable `Demo Mode` in `Settings`.
- Confirm Council name and reserves policy.
- Open Dashboard.

## 1. Executive Snapshot (60 seconds)
- Point out the S151 Summary strip (RAG, 4-year gap, final reserves).
- Highlight “Scenario Story: Base vs Pessimistic”.

## 2. Assumptions & Sensitivity (90 seconds)
- Adjust 1–2 assumptions in the sidebar (council tax, pay award).
- Show instant change in gap and RAG.
- Open `Sensitivity` to show top drivers (tornado chart).

## 3. Risk Advisor (60 seconds)
- Open `Risk Advisor`.
- Show linked risk register and composite stress scenario.
- Apply a stress (optional), return to dashboard.

## 4. Governance Evidence (90 seconds)
- Open `Reports`.
- Generate `Executive Summary` (1 page).
- Generate `Board Pack` (multi-page).
- Show audit trail in `Audit`.

## 5. Close (30 seconds)
- Summarize: “Transparent assumptions, evidence-ready outputs, governance-grade audit trail.”
- Offer pilot: “We can onboard one council dataset in 1–2 weeks.”
