# MTFS Budget Simulator: Demo Script for Section 151 Officer

## 1. Introduction & Setup (2 minutes)

*   **Objective:** "Good morning/afternoon. Today, I'll walk you through the MTFS Budget Simulator, a decision-support tool designed to give you real-time insights into your Medium-Term Financial Strategy."
*   **Key Benefit:** "It's built to provide instant clarity on how strategic decisions around funding, expenditure, and capital investment will impact your budget gap and financial resilience."
*   **Initial View:**
    *   Show the main dashboard.
    *   Point out the clean, professional layout designed for senior stakeholders.
    *   Highlight the key KPIs at the top: Sustainability RAG rating, 4-Year Funding Gap, and Final Reserves. "This gives you an immediate, at-a-glance health check of your MTFS."

## 2. Core MTFS Modelling (5 minutes)

*   **Scenario:** "Let's start by modelling a baseline scenario."
*   **Adjusting Assumptions:**
    *   Use the sidebar sliders to adjust core assumptions.
    *   "Here on the left, you have full control over the key drivers of your MTFS."
    *   Change the 'Council Tax Increase' slider. "Watch how the funding gap and reserves update instantly as I adjust the council tax assumption. No waiting, no re-running spreadsheets."
    *   Make a change to 'Adult Social Care Demand Growth'. "Let's model an increase in ASC demand. You can immediately see the pressure it puts on your expenditure and the overall gap."
*   **Explaining the Visuals:**
    *   Point to the "Budget Gap by Year" chart. "This chart visualizes the trajectory of your budget gap over the 4-year period, helping you see when the pressures will be most acute."
    *   Scroll to the "Reserves Depletion Profile". "And this shows the corresponding impact on your reserves, with a clear minimum threshold marker so you can ensure you're meeting your policy targets."
*   **Quick Scenarios:**
    *   Use the "Optimistic" and "Pessimistic" buttons. "To speed things up, we have pre-configured scenarios. Let's apply a pessimistic outlook... and you can see the immediate impact on the RAG rating and the funding gap."

## 3. NEW: Capital Programme Impact (5 minutes)

*   **Introducing the Feature:** "Now, let's look at a powerful new feature: modelling the revenue impact of your capital programme."
*   **Navigate to Capital Projects Page (or section):** "We'll move to the capital projects section."
*   **Add a New Capital Project:**
    *   "Imagine you're considering a new £15m leisure centre project."
    *   Enter `15,000,000` into the 'Capital Project Value' input.
    *   Select 'Borrowing' as the financing method.
    *   "The tool automatically calculates the annual MRP and interest costs based on the asset life and interest rate assumptions."
*   **Show the Revenue Impact:**
    *   Point out the "Annual Revenue Financing Cost" output. "Here is the new annual revenue cost of that project: £Xm."
*   **Connect to MTFS:**
    *   Go back to the main dashboard.
    *   Point to the "Revenue Impact of Capital Programme" panel. "This new panel clearly summarizes the total revenue impact from all your capital projects."
    *   "Most importantly, the main budget gap has been automatically updated to include these new costs. You can see the 'before' and 'after' impact, giving you a true picture of affordability."
*   **The "Bridge" Waterfall:**
    *   Show the "Capital Financing Revenue Impact Bridge". "This waterfall chart provides a simple, powerful visual story. It starts with your initial budget gap, adds the pressure from MRP and interest, and shows you the final, revised gap. It makes the impact of capital decisions incredibly clear for all stakeholders."

## 4. Resilience & Governance (2 minutes)

*   **CIPFA Resilience Indicators:**
    *   "The KPIs and metrics in this tool are aligned with the CIPFA Financial Resilience Index."
    *   Point to indicators like 'Financing costs to net revenue stream' or 'Change in reserves'. "This ensures the outputs directly support your formal resilience assessments and annual governance statements."
*   **Audit & Export:**
    *   Show the 'Audit' page. "Every assumption change and scenario you run is logged, providing a full audit trail for governance."
    *   Click the 'Generate PDF Report' button. "Finally, you can export a clean, board-ready PDF summary of your chosen scenario with a single click, ready for your leadership team or committee papers."

## 5. Summary & Next Steps (1 minute)

*   **Recap:** "So, in just a few minutes, we've modelled multiple MTFS scenarios, assessed the revenue impact of a major capital project, and seen how it all aligns with your financial resilience metrics."
*   **Value Proposition:** "This tool empowers you to make faster, more confident strategic decisions, backed by transparent, real-time data."
*   **Close:** "I'd be happy to answer any questions."
